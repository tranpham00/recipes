Baked Apple

Description

Baked apples are soft and sweet, and quick and easy to make.

Prepared stuffed or sliced, it's easy to make as a quick treat for the holidays.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 large baking apples (like honeycrisp)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup lightly crushed nuts of choice
  • 1/4 cup chopped dried fruit of choice (optional)
  • 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom
  • 1-2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. heat the oven - place the rack in the center of the oven and preheat to 375 F
  2. make sugar/cinnamon mixture - combine sugar, nuts, fruit, cinnamon, and cardamom in small bowl. mix well
  3. core the apples - use an apple corer or sharp paring knife to remove the core, making room for the mixture
  4. stuff the apples - arrange the apples in a baking dish, spoon in the brown sugar and nut mixture all the way
  5. add the oil and hot water - pour about 1-2 tbsp of oil all over apples. pour 3/4-1 cup of boiling water from the side of the dish
  6. bake - place the dish in the oven and bake for 40-45 minutes or until cooked through and tender
  7. serve - remove from oven, baste with brown sugar sauce from bottom of pan. serve as is or add vanilla ice cream and serve.
